New Comers:

For the new comers to this custom mechenical keyboard world, I recommend you to buy the folloing:
(And you should also have a soldering iron in hand)
  • Switch: the main triggering mechenism that do the key registering
  • Stablier: mainly used on the longer keys, such as shift/ space/ delete
  • Cable: to connect the pcb, and you may burn the chip for a different key configureation
  • Plate: used to hold switches in place
  • PCB: the main brain sending signal to your computer
  • Case: where all the above sits in.
